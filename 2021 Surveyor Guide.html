Overview

Model: 2021 Surveyor Legend 320BHLE Travel Trailer (37 feet)

Key Features:

  • Length: 37 feet
  • Dry Weight: 7,300 lbs
  • Sleeping Capacity: Up to 10 people
  • Outdoor Kitchen: Mini-fridge, grill, and sink
  • Spacious living and dining area with U-shaped dinette
  • Full bathroom with shower, toilet, and sink

Common Issues

Like most RVs, the 2021 Surveyor Legend 320BHLE has some common issues:

  • Water Leaks Around Slide-Outs: Make sure to inspect seals regularly.
  • Trim and Finish Issues: Watch for loose trim after initial use.
  • Electrical Problems: Some users report issues with outlets and the fridge, usually due to breaker trips.

Power Setup with Honda Generator

To mitigate power limitations at 30-amp campsites, a Honda EU7000iS Generator is recommended:

  • Power Output: 7,000 watts starting, 5,500 watts running
  • Noise Level: 52 dBA – super quiet
  • Fuel Efficiency: Up to 18 hours on a single tank of gasoline
  • Price: $4,500 - $5,000

This generator is perfect for running high-draw appliances like air conditioners, refrigerators, and microwaves. With a quiet operation, it’s campground-friendly.

Setup Includes:

  • Automatic Transfer Switch (ATS) to switch between shore and generator power: $250 - $350
  • Power Management System to prevent overloading circuits: $350 - $450
  • 30-Amp to 50-Amp adapter for compatibility: $15 - $30

Maintenance Tips

  • Inspect and reseal roof annually, especially around slide-outs.
  • Lubricate slide-out mechanisms and inspect rubber seals regularly.
  • Check tire pressure and tread before every trip.
  • Flush water heater and sanitize freshwater tanks regularly.
  • Run and inspect all major appliances before trips (A/C, fridge, microwave).
